Services

Reliance Innovations Group provides comprehensive services to landlords, ensuring the maintenance and safety of their properties. Our system, designed for after every short-term renter turnover, includes a thorough inspection of various aspects of the property:

  1. Structural Integrity: We inspect walls, doors, cabinets, and windows for any signs of damage or wear that may require attention.
  2. Plumbing and Utilities: All plumbing is checked for leaks or clogs, including the water heater. We ensure that the property is equipped with proper functioning utilities.
  3. Electrical Fixtures: Lights and electrical fixtures are inspected to ensure they are in good working condition and pose no safety hazards.
  4. Appliances: We check all appliances to ensure they are in working condition, including refrigerators, stoves, ovens, microwaves, etc.
  5. Landscaping: Our team checks the landscaping and tends to the plants to maintain the aesthetic appeal of the property.
  6. Pool and Hot Tubs: Pools and hot tubs are inspected, cleaned, and maintained to ensure they are safe for use.
  7. Security: Security cameras and locks are checked to ensure they are operational and provide adequate security for the property and guests.
  8. Quality Assurance: After completing the inspection, we take pictures and recordings to ensure quality assurance for the following guests. This also serves as proof in case any damages occur during subsequent stays.
  9. Specialized Insurance: Reliance Innovations Group offers multiple specialized insurance policies that cover up to $1 million in damages, providing landlords with peace of mind and financial protection.

By providing these comprehensive services, we aim to assist landlords in maintaining their properties' condition, ensuring guest satisfaction, and minimizing the risk of any potential damages.

Reliance Innovations Group is committed to securing the property of landlords through the latest in technology and innovative solutions. Our approach involves a multi-layered security system that ensures the safety and privacy of guests while protecting the landlord's investment.

  1. Ring Doorbells and Floodlight Cameras: Every front door that is wired will be equipped with the newest Ring doorbells, providing real-time video and audio surveillance of who is entering the property. In addition, Ring floodlight cameras will be installed around the property's entry points, ensuring comprehensive coverage and a deterrent to potential intruders.
  2. Privacy Considerations: For legal reasons and to respect the privacy of our guests, we do not install cameras within the building or facing the backyard. However, our cameras are strategically placed to capture all individuals entering the premises, ensuring the safety and security of the property.
  3. Smart Locks: The newest smart locks will be installed at every entrance door, offering ease of entry for guests and allowing for automatic resetting after each guest's stay. This ensures that only authorized individuals have access to the property.
  4. Soundware and Smoke Alarms: Our advanced soundware measures decibels within the building, deterring unwanted parties and alerting us to any suspicious activity. Smoke alarms are installed in every room, providing protection against fire hazards and alerting us if anyone is smoking within the building.
  5. Secure Wi-Fi and Router: A fast and secure Wi-Fi connection is essential for the proper functioning of all devices and systems. Our secure Wi-Fi and router ensure that the property's technology remains uninterrupted and free from tampering.
  6. Latest Technology: We pride ourselves on using the latest technology to protect both guests and the landlord's investment. Our comprehensive security system combines the latest in surveillance, access control, and soundware to provide a safe and secure environment for all.

At Reliance Innovations Group, we understand the importance of security and privacy for both guests and landlords. Our cutting-edge security measures ensure peace of mind and protection for all parties involved.

Reliance Innovations Group is committed to ensuring that only trustworthy guests are allowed to stay in the properties we manage. Our rigorous guest vetting process is designed to provide peace of mind to landlords and create a safe and enjoyable experience for all guests.

  1. Identity Verification: We require guests to provide a valid government-issued photo ID and additional verification, such as a credit card or utility bill, to confirm their identity. This helps us ensure that guests are who they claim to be.
  2. Rental History and Reviews: We review guests' rental history and any reviews they may have received from previous hosts. This allows us to assess their behavior and reliability as guests.
  3. Background Checks: In some cases, we may conduct background checks on guests to screen for criminal history or other relevant information.
  4. Communication and Responsiveness: We assess guests' communication and responsiveness during the booking process. Prompt and clear communication is an indicator of a responsible guest.
  5. Purpose of Stay: We ask guests to provide information about the purpose of their stay, such as vacation, business, or event attendance. This helps us understand their intentions and ensure that the property is suitable for their needs.
  6. House Rules Agreement: We require guests to agree to our house rules, which outline expectations for behavior, cleanliness, and property use. This ensures that guests are aware of and agree to abide by our policies.
  7. Security Deposit: We may require guests to pay a security deposit, which can be used to cover any damages or violations of the house rules.
  8. Guest Screening: Our team reviews all guest information and makes a final determination on whether to accept or decline the booking. We prioritize the safety and comfort of our landlords and guests.

At Reliance Innovations Group, we believe that a thorough guest vetting process is essential for creating a positive and secure rental experience. Our goal is to provide landlords with peace of mind and ensure that their properties are well cared for by responsible and respectful guests.
